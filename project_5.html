VJing

Below are pieces I created during the Roundhouse Young Creatives VJing course. We experimented with the MadMapper software to create new visuals, edit existing videos, and project them in collaboration with live music.

I really enjoyed being able to edit and distort existing videos in connection with music. I found the process similar to collaging, collecting and arranging different forms of media with different shapes , patterns and colours.

Unfortunately due to my computer's processing power, some of the videos below are lagging. With a higher processing speed these would be a lot smoother.

Please enjoy the pieces with music by clicking the sound icon in the corner.
